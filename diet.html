Veg Plan (Mon–Sat)

Early Morning: 5 soaked almonds, 2 walnuts, 1 date, warm lemon water
Breakfast: Oats with milk or paneer bhurji + roti
Mid-Morning: Dry fruits + fruit
Lunch: Brown rice/2 chapatis + dal + sabzi + curd + salad
Evening: Sprouts chaat or paneer cubes + fruit
Pre-Workout: Banana or dates
Post-Workout: Milk/protein + sweet potato
Dinner: Dal/veg sabzi + salad + curd
Before Bed: Warm milk with turmeric

Non-Veg Plan (Mon–Sat)

Early Morning: 5 almonds + warm water
Breakfast: 3 boiled eggs + rotis or oats + milk
Mid-Morning: Dry fruits + fruit
Lunch: Rice/chapati + 150g grilled chicken/fish + dal + salad
Evening: Boiled egg or roasted chana + green tea
Pre-Workout: Banana + peanut butter
Post-Workout: Protein shake or boiled eggs
Dinner: Grilled chicken/fish + salad + curd
Before Bed: Milk with turmeric
